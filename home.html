About Our Municipality

History & Heritage

The Municipality of Calape was formally established in 1802. Its name is linked to "kalapi," a rattan type once abundant here, suggesting a history rich in craftsmanship. The town is also home to the San Vicente Ferrer Parish Church, a stunning Gothic-inspired structure and a designated National Cultural Treasure.

Geography & Demographics

  • Location: 41.4 km from Tagbilaran City on the western coast of Bohol.
  • Area: 75.36 square kilometers across 33 barangays.
  • Population: 33,079 residents (2020 Census).
